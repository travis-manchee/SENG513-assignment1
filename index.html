About Me and EZ Recipes

My Name is Travis Manchee, I am a software engineer undergrad at the University of Calgary's Schulich School of Engineering. EZ Recipes is my submission for SENG513 assignment 1. I chose to create EZ Recipes because like many students, I love to eat amazing homemade food but generally lack the experience and skills required for many recipes. As a result I have come to collect recipes that are both easy to make and well received at dinner parties.
