Garlic Butter Baked Brie

Cheese lovers rejoice, there is nothing simpler than a baked brie. This recipe is quick to make and great to serve with bread, crackers, or chips. Serve warm for the ultimate gooey cheesy experience!

Servings: 6 Servings

Ingredients

  • 1 round of brie (8-12oz 22-340g)
  • 2 garlic cloves, minced
  • 2 tablespoons butter, melted
  • 1/2 teaspoon thyme leaves
  • black pepper (to taste)

Directions

  1. Pre-heat oven to 375F (190C).
  2. Score top of the brie with 1/2" cuts in crosshatch. Place brie in small oven safe ceramic dish.
  3. Combine minced garlic with melted butter, pour mixture over brie.
  4. Sprinkle thyme leaves and fresh cracked black pepper.
  5. Bake for about 10-15 minutes, or until brie is melted.

Truffled Deviled Eggs

Deviled eggs is a classic appetizer that pairs nicely with a splash of truffle oil. The recipe is perfectly scalable for any dinner party, simply choose how many eggs you wish to make and scale all ingredients accordingly.

Servings: 6 Servings (12 egg halves)

Ingredients

  • 6 Large hard-boiled eggs, halved
  • 1/4 cup mayonnaise
  • 1 teaspoon rice wine vinegar
  • 1/2 teaspoon fresh dill, chopped
  • 1 teaspoon dijon mustard
  • 1/4 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon truffle oil
  • salt and pepper (to taste)

Directions

  1. Scoop egg yolks into medium mixing bowl setting whites aside.
  2. Add mayonnaise, vinegar, dill, mustard, garlic powder and 1/4 teaspoon truffle oil to the bowl, mashing until fully mixed.
  3. Salt and pepper mixture to taste.
  4. Spoon mixture into egg white halves. placing filled halves onto serving plate.
  5. Lightly drizzle 1/4 teaspoon truffle oil over deviled eggs
  6. Cover serving plate with cling wrap and refrigerate until ready to serve.
